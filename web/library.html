🧅 Onion Loop — the library

The whole idea in one picture. Permissions live on the original books. An AI summary inherits from whichever books it was made from — so if you can't read a book, you can't read a summary of it. Shred a book and its summaries vanish for everyone. Every decision lands in a hash-chained receipt nobody can quietly rewrite.
