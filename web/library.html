🧅 Onion Loop — the library

Permissions live on the original books. An AI summary inherits from whichever books it was made from — so to read a summary you must be allowed to read every book behind it. Shred a book and its summaries vanish for everyone. Every decision lands in a hash-chained receipt nobody can quietly rewrite.
